Ceremony. Chapel of the Incarnate Word.

The wedding ceremony will take place at the Chapel of the Incarnate Word at 3:00pm on November 21, 2015. The procession starts promptly at 3:00pm, so please try to be there early. You won't want to miss Heather walking down the aisle! :D

Reception. The St. Anthony Hotel

The reception will be held immediately after the ceremony at The St. Anthony Hotel in beautiful downtown San Antonio. Cocktails and Hors D'Oeuvres will be served from 4:30 to 5:45pm, dinner will be served at 6:00pm. Please RSVP below.

Event parking is available at The St. Anthony Hotel parking lot on the corner of Jefferson and E Pecan for $10, or $15 for Valet.

We recommend that you stay downtown, especially if you're drinking. We have a discounted room rate for the St. Anthony Hotel. Just call the service desk (210-227-4392) to book your room before October 20, and ask for the "Speck and Mendizabal Courtesy Block". Other great options would be the Home2 Suites by Hilton or Townplace Suites. Both are within walking distance of the St. Anthony.
